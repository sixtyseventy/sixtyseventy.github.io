Shutdown Notice

ph-science is shutting down

We’ve made the tough decision to shut down ph-science for good.

This wasn’t easy. Not long ago, the game supplier made some big changes, especially locking down their JSON files and APIs. Because of that, we can’t keep the platform working the way it’s supposed to, and we’re not able to update or improve it anymore. So, we’ve decided to officially end development.

We know this sucks. ph-science wasn’t just a project—it was a place where people could explore, learn, and hang out. What made it special was the community behind it.

Even though this is the end of ph-science, we’re not done creating. We’re now working on something new: a movie site. The goal is to build a fresh experience with new ways to be creative and connect. We’ll share more about it soon.

There’s also a chance the forums might come back later, possibly with new features like image sharing, voice messages, and better ways to interact. Nothing is confirmed yet, but it’s something we’re considering.

Thank you to everyone who was part of this. Whether you helped build it, joined conversations, or just spent time on the site—you made it what it was. That really meant a lot to us.

It’s kind of crazy to think about—ph-science only started on March 4 and ended on April 15. It didn’t last long, but it still meant something.
