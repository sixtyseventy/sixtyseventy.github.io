Shutdown Notice

ph-science is closing permanently

Unfortunately, we have reached a difficult decision regarding ph-science closure.

In recent months, the game provider introduced drastic changes which resulted in locking access to their JSON files and APIs. This prevents any further work in order to maintain and expand the website's functionality.

Therefore, we will be ceasing our development activities and closing ph-science down for good.

It goes without saying that this decision will have its cons – we had fun exploring, chatting, and making games at ph-science. It provided a unique opportunity to socialize and enjoy the company. Of course, all this would not be possible without the community.

While ph-science is coming to an end, the development is not stopping. We currently focus our efforts on creating another project – a movie site with innovative functionality and creative possibilities.

Moreover, there's a possibility of reopening the ph-science forums sometime in the future along with the additional features such as image upload, voice messaging and improved communication.

Thanks to all those people who participated in building ph-science community in any form – you made this platform unique in its essence.

It's strange to think how fast everything happened – ph-science started on March 4 and ended on April 15, 2026. Short but real.
