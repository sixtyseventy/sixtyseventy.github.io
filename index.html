Shutdown Notice

ph-science will officially be shutting down permanently

After a long period of development, updates, and support from the community. This decision wasn’t made lightly. Due to major changes from the game supplier—especially the privatization of their JSON files and APIs—the platform is no longer able to function in the way it was originally designed. These changes have made it impossible to maintain key features and continue improving the experience. Because of this, development on the current version has officially come to an end.

We understand that this may come as disappointing news. ph-science has been more than just a project—it’s been a place where people explored, learned, and connected. Every update, feature, and discussion was shaped by the people who used it, and that made it something truly special.

Looking ahead, the focus will now shift to a new and exciting direction. The only active project moving forward will be an upcoming movie site, which aims to bring a fresh experience and new opportunities for creativity and engagement. More details about this project will be shared over time.

There is also a possibility that the forums could return in the future. If they do, they may include new and improved features such as image sharing, voice recordings, and additional ways to communicate and collaborate with others across different classes and communities. While nothing is guaranteed yet, the idea is still being considered.

Thank you to everyone who has been part of this journey. Whether you contributed, participated in discussions, or simply spent time on the platform, you helped make it what it was. Your support, feedback, and presence meant a lot and will not be forgotten.
