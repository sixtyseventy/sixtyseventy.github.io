Shutdown Notice

ph-science is closing permanently.

In recent months, the game provider introduced drastic changes which resulted in locking access to their JSON files and APIs. This prevents any further work to maintain and expand the website's functionality.

Therefore, we will be ceasing our development activities and closing ph-science down for good.

We had fun exploring, chatting, and making games at ph-science. It provided a unique opportunity to socialize and enjoy the company — none of which would be possible without the community.

While ph-science is coming to an end, the development is not stopping. We currently focus our efforts on creating another project — a movie site with innovative functionality and creative possibilities.

There's also a possibility of reopening the ph-science forums in the future, with image upload, voice messaging, and improved communication.

Thanks to all those who participated in building the ph-science community in any form — you made this platform unique.

It's strange to think how fast everything happened. Short but real.
